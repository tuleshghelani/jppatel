WELCOME TO J.P. PATEL

J.P.PATEL is a leading Manufacturer, Supplier and Wholesaler of Silver Ornaments in Pan India.. Our company was established in the year 2001 at Rajkot (Gujarat). With time, our merchandise has grown extremely wide as we showcase high standards of quality designs that satisfy every style need of customers.

"J.P.PATEL" has the finest manufacturing units within India at Rajkot (A major Jewellery manufacturing hub).

We use a perfect blend of in-house designer team and external consultants which makes us proficient enough to provide an incomparable range of silver Jewellery. We create wide variety of silver ornaments like Bichhiya, Payal (Anklets) Guchha, Kardan, Sterling Silver Jewellery, Pendants, Ring, Toe-Ring, Necklaces, Chain, Bracelets and Bangles in almost every kind of design and style.
